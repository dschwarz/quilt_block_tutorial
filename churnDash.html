fabric quantity dimensions color
strips 1 2.5 x 10 both colors

Step 1

Make points

  • Place two different colored point squares on top of each other, right sides facing. Align the sides and corners.
  • Stitch a diagonal line from one point to another, across the center.
  • Fold the resulting triangle over and press.
  • Trim seam allowance. You will be trimming of a triangle from both fabrics.
  • Repeat for three remaining corners.

Step 2

Make square units from strips

  • Stitch the two different colored strips to each other along the 10in. side, right sides facing.
  • Press open
  • Using a rotary cutter and ruler, cut the sewn strip into 2.5in pieces along the 10in side. You will have 4 pieces

Step 3

Assemble rows

  • For row 1: Sew a triangle unit to a strip unit to a triangle unit.
  • For row 2: Sew a strip unit to the center square to a strip unit.
  • For row 3: Sew a triangle unit to a strip unit to a triangle unit.

Step 4

Sew rows together

  • Sew row 1 to row 2
  • Sew row 2 to row 3
  • Press seams open.
