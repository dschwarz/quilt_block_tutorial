Ohio Star

tutorial

Cut

fabric quantity dimensions
center 1 6.5 x 6.5
points 8 3.5 x 3.5
background 4 3.5 x 3.5
background 4 6.5 x 3.5

Step 1

Make points

  • Place a point square on top of a background rectangle, right sides facing. Align the square with one end of the rectangle.
  • Stitch a diagonal line from the outer corner of the point square in toward the center.
  • Fold the resulting triangle over and press.
  • Trim seam allowance. You will be trimming of a triangle from both fabrics.
  • Repeat for other corner and other background rectangles.

Step 2

Sew block rows

  • For row 1, sew a background square to either short side of the rectangle with the points. Make sure the points point up.
  • For row 2, sew the long side of a background rectangle to each side of the center square. Make sure the points point out.
  • For row 3, sew a background square to either short side of the rectangle with the points. Make sure the points point down.
  • Press seams open.

Step 3

Sew rows together

  • Sew row 1 to row 2
  • Sew row 2 to row 3
  • Press seams open.
